Detailed Description

Entry point for the Gazebo robotic arm joint controller.

Implements a state-machine-based controller that sequences a 3-DOF robotic arm through a series of world-space targets using PID-controlled joint velocities and inverse kinematics solved per target.

Rotate joint0 (base) to face the current target.

Extend joint1 and joint2 to reach the target position.

Return joint1 and joint2 to the home (retracted) position.

WAIT
