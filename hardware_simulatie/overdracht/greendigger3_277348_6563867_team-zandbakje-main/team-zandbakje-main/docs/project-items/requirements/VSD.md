# Value Sensitive Design

`Authors: Ocarian, Replitard, GrijzePanda, ShelleKjell` 

## Key Information
- **Value Sensitive Design (VSD)**, is a proactive approach to creating products, services, and systems that embed moral, social, and ethical principles directly into the design process. Instead of treating ethics as a constraint added at the end, this approach considers values like privacy, justice, sustainability, and autonomy from the very beginning
- When designing an application, it's important to keep collateral in mind. Such as stakeholders you didn't know where relevant.
